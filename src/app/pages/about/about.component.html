About PET-EGYPT

PET for packaging Materials is a manufacture of wide array of PET containers and merchandise, PET-EGYPT is well structured to support large users of thermoformed packaging and carries forward the enhanced optical and mechanical properties of virgin & recycle PET.

Our vision

To be the leader in providing packaging solution & to be customer first choice in harmony with profitable growth.

Our mission

To be the leader in the field of plastic packaging industry (food & beverage division) through building upon our existing business in the market segments where we have the opportunity to become the recognized leader and through application of emerging technology to manufacture innovative products that protect & promote out customer's product.

Our value integrity

Honesty & fairness are essential to the way we do business & interact with people. We shall consistently treat our customers, employees, shareholders, suppliers & community with honesty, dignity, fairness & respect. We shall conduct our business with the highest ethical standard.

Excellence

Our pursuit of excellence leads us to new levels of professionalism. To be excellent, we must challenge ourselves to exceed the expectations of our customers, shareholders & colleagues by striving to surpass our targets and improve our result.

Responsibility

PET works to empower every individual at every level of our organization through enhancing their capabilities to take the responsibility with full reliability.
