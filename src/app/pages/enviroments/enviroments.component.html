environment

We in PET for packaging Materials trying to Save Our Planet , Through the years, we have been a leader in managing our business in a way that impacts the environment in a friendly, positive, and thoughtful manner. Long before others were paying attention to environmental issues, we implemented the following Environmental Vision and Management Policy:

Saving Light Energy

Electricity is one of the most important factor in any industry and in our life most of countries in third world are suffering from electricity shortage due to shortage of natural resources as Petroleum or water falls.

PET uses intelligent roof design with smart sky light panel inside all production & warehouse areas that allow working using direct sun light for more than 10 hours daily.

Clear & Green air-conditioning system

Plastic Industry in general need high temperature in all production processes therefore it generates hot temperature inside production hall which create non comfort atmosphere for employee and bad conditions for machines & materials.
PET uses the most intelligent and green air condition system worldwide using natural air flow & natural water in closed loop, without using any Energy or any type of Gas We can keep inside temperature up to 26 degree 12 month year.
